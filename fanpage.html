Bio

Joji, aka George Miller, started his career as a youtuber doing satire videos and comedic songs. His comedy ranged from cooking ramen in his bathtub and harassing people on the street, to cooking literal dead street rats dressed as Guy Fieri and jokes about suicide. His health began to deteriorate and he decided to put more energy into making the music he really wanted to make, while still maintaining his irreverent attitude. In 2017 he released his first album under the 88rising music label under his new name, and has been dropping bangers ever since.
